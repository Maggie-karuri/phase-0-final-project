Project 2: Speed Detector

The speed calculator is used to assess whether the speed is past the allowed limit, and if the speed passes the limit, the driver receives semerit points. Also if they exceed certain points, their driving license is revoked.

In this project, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is used to create the program. I used If---else statements to present the different scenarios.

  • The first condition is that the maximumm allowed speed is 70km/hr.
  • The second condition is that for every additional 5km/hr, the driver is awarded 1 demerit point.
  • third condition is that if the driver exceeds 12 demerit points, their license is revoked.
